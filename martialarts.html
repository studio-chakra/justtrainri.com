It doesn’t matter, as long as you’re here I’m sure we have something to offer you.

It can be very intimidating to walk in to a Martial Arts school and start training, especially if you have never trained before. One thing you’ll find missing at Just Train is the ego you can sometimes find in the martial arts world. Instead, what you’ll notice here is that we are all a team and we are all on the floor to help each other get better and evolve as martial artists. One thing about our martial arts programs that I feel is important to convey to people right of the bat is that everything we teach comes from a self defense perspective. So we will show you the art as it is intended, with preservation of the art in mind, but we will also show you how it can be used in a modern world. How it can be used specifically by you with your body type and abilities. Teaching people martial arts is a huge responsibility that does not go unappreciated by us. Our instructors and assistants are all of the same mind and that is to give you what you need to do your best to prevail in a violent encounter.

Whatever art your training in, whether it’s Bruce Lee’s Jeet Kune Do, the devastating martial art of Muay Thai, Filipino martial arts, or all of the above you can take what you learn in those arts and plug them into our training program called Force Necessary Practical Application. In the “Prac. App.” classes we take it up a notch to whatever level you are comfortable with to safely “pressure test” the techniques and find out what works for you, which is something you want to find out in training and not in a real time violent encounter.
Something else you’ll find at Just Train is an open mind when it come to cross training… we encourage it!! Throughout the year we will have some of the best martial artists and violence experts IN THE WORLD teaching at our facility. Giving us another dimension to our training that most other schools just don’t offer.
As you’ll see repeated around here often….show up, step on the floor and JUST TRAIN.

If you want to see what we’re up to check us out on Facebook, shoot me an email with questions or just stop by check us out.

See you on the mat,
(Sifu) Chris
