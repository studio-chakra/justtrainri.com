Looking to push it to another level? Get Superhero Strong

Functional Fitness Interval Training is the class for you. Strong is the new fit. Not just strong but functionally strong, strong for your everyday life. Get that “Superhero” feeling you get from breaking through physical plateaus, learning to move properly and with agility, plus be strong and stable in any position. These classes will be semi-private, no more than 6 people per class. You will be monitored closely with all your goals and stats recorded. Improve your functional strength, speed, reaction time, and ability to avoid injury because your body is balanced and strong.You’re going to be swinging kettlebells, whipping battle ropes, band work, agility ladders, and Plyo boxes. You will be jumping, pulling, pushing, dragging and lifting your way to a level of fitness you thought impossible. But it isn’t impossible, not with with us.
